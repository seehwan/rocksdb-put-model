Related Work

This section summarizes prior work closely related to our project on RocksDB put-rate modeling, compaction-constrained throughput, write amplification, and stability under stalls. We prioritize top systems/database venues (SOSP/NSDI/FAST/ATC, SIGMOD/VLDB/PACMMOD, ASPLOS, CACM).

Foundations & Surveys

Core LSM concepts and broad surveys that ground our modeling choices.

  1. O'Neil, Patrick, Cheng, et al. (1996). "The log-structured merge-tree (LSM-tree)". Acta Informatica. link
  2. Luo, Chen, Carey, et al. (2020). "LSM-based Storage Techniques: A Survey". The VLDB Journal.
  3. Liu, Junfeng, Wang, et al. (2024). "Structural Designs Meet Optimality: Exploring Optimized LSM-tree Structures in a Colossal Configuration Space". Proceedings of the ACM on Management of Data. link

Compaction Theory & Tuning

Analytical and empirical work on compaction layouts, parameters, and cost trade-offs.

  1. Sears, Russell, Ramakrishnan, et al. (2012). "bLSM: A General Purpose Log Structured Merge Tree". Proceedings of the 2012 ACM SIGMOD International Conference on Management of Data.
  2. Dayan, Niv, Athanassoulis, et al. (2017). "Monkey: Optimal Navigable Key-Value Store". Proceedings of the 2017 {ACM} {SIGMOD} International Conference on Management of Data.
  3. Ren, Kai, Zheng, et al. (2017). "SlimDB: A Space-Efficient Key-Value Storage Engine for Semi-Sorted Data". Proceedings of the VLDB Endowment.

Write Amplification & KV-Separation

Work on reducing write amplification through key-value separation and other techniques.

  1. Kang, Woon-Hak, Keeton, et al. (2013). "WiscKey: Separating Keys from Values in SSD-Conscious Storage". ACM Transactions on Storage.
  2. Lu, Lanyue, Pillai, et al. (2016). "WiscKey: Separating Keys from Values in SSD-Conscious Storage". Proceedings of the 14th USENIX Conference on File and Storage Technologies.

Stability, Stall & Scheduling

Research on LSM-tree stability, write stalls, and scheduling policies.

  1. Dayan, Niv, Idreos, et al. (2018). "Dostoevsky: Better Space-Time Trade-Offs for LSM-Tree Based Key-Value Stores via Adaptive Removal of Superfluous Merging". Proceedings of the 2018 {ACM} {SIGMOD} International Conference on Management of Data.

Workload Modeling & Production Systems

Studies of real-world workloads and production system behavior.

  1. Luo, Siqiang, Chatterjee, et al. (2020). "Rosetta: A Robust Space-Time Optimized Range Filter for Key-Value Stores". Proceedings of the 2020 {ACM} {SIGMOD} International Conference on Management of Data.
